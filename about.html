I am a fourth year Victoria University student studying a Bachelor of Design Innovation, majoring in Media Design. My long-term career goal is to become a Game Design Modeler and Animator.

My strengths are in good team-work skills, dedication to learn any necessary skills that are needed for a job and my determination to achieve highly in my work.
